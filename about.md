# About

I'm Rob Kirby. I live near Oxford and work on applying AI in software organisations, with a focus on LLMs, workflows, and how teams change the way they operate.

Before software I was an Intelligence Corps officer in the British Army. Before that I studied philosophy. The through-line is probably something about information, judgment, and how to make sense of change.

This blog is where I write about things I'm noticing. I also have a podcast called [Liminal Time](https://liminaltimepodcast.com). See also: [what I'm doing now](/now/).
